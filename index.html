Improved town and battle AI

More reasonable building orders. Better planned resources. Attack only where you can win. AI now can be more challengeable to player than ever.

Night lights

Too dark at night? Bring small point lights in buildings. Move your troops through natural darkness, where opponent can't see.

A* Pathfinding Project extension

Start using "A* Pathfinding Project" paths and avoidances. Enable it on procedurally generated endless terrain. Move thousands of units as far as the eye can see.

Procedural fires

Spread procedural fires along building walls. Animate fire arrow smoke trails and sparks.

Time of the day

Enable day and night cycles based on real world rotations. Bring it for various latitudes.

Animated characters and LODs

All animated characters were modelled using Blender. They have multiple LODs. Finally simple LOD system was created to address rendering and optimisations and bring richer variety of characters.

uRTS becomes multiplayer

uRTS finally makes multiplayer mode real. Initial phase starts with very simple LAN configuration of host and client, but "multiplayered" various areas allow to enjoy large variety of RTS features together with your friends.

3dSprites has been released and featured

It is time to break deep integration, unclearness and cluttering once more. 3dSprites becomes a separate asset openning opportunities to render thousands of objects for anyone.
